# Project Blueprint: 닭갈비가 싸닭 (Dakgalbi-ga Ssadak)

## Overview

Single-page franchise inquiry site for "닭갈비가 싸닭" with a strong operational focus: clear benefits, support, process, and inquiry flow, plus a delivery-first video section that highlights realistic store operations.

## Design and Style Guide

*   **Aesthetics:** Operational, trustworthy, and modern.
*   **Color Palette:** Dark neutral base with warm orange highlights for emphasis.
*   **Typography:** Clean sans-serif for body copy, strong headline sizing for key messages.
*   **Imagery:** Placeholder hero background image and a video placeholder box for the delivery-focused section.
*   **Layout:** Single-page layout with stacked sections and responsive grids.

## Current Implementation Outline

*   **Header:** Sticky header with brand logo and navigation anchors.
*   **Hero:** Full-screen background image, franchise headline, CTA buttons, and quick badges.
*   **Delivery Video Section:** Delivery-focused recruitment message, video placeholder, concept checklist, main copy and subcopy, plus an operational summary block (3 cards).
*   **Why Franchise:** Four cards describing operational strengths.
*   **Head Office Support:** Four support items.
*   **Process:** Four-step ordered list.
*   **Costs:** Simple cost table.
*   **Inquiry:** Contact form and direct contact details.
*   **Footer:** Brand copyright.
